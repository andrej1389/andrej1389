OVERVIEW

Coffeeshop Istanbul is a concept responsive website created during my study at Google course ( my first project ). Coffeeshop Istanbul is an online store that offers a wide range high-quality coffees from different parts of the world.

MY ROLE

In this project, i served as a sole designer, encompassing all phases of the process - from user research to final user experience and interface design. My role was comprehensive, covering research, conceptualization, prototype design and implementation, allowing me to shape every aspect of the project with care and dedication.

PROBLEM STATEMENT

The current landscape of online coffee purchasing lacks a centralized platform that effectively showcases and offers a diverse selection of high-quality coffees from various regions worldwide. This absence results in a fragmented user experience, making it challenging for coffee enthusiasts to explore and purchase their desired coffee varieties easily and efficiently.

GOAL

The goal is to create an intuitive and user-friendly online platform for Coffeeshop Istanbul that provides, explore and purchase a diverse range of high-quality coffees from around the world. This platform aims to enhance user engagement and satisfaction through tailored user experiences and comprehensive product information, ultimately fostering long-term customer relationships and brand loyalty.

USER RESEARCH

Assumptions after research: After conducting the research, it was found that there is a significant demand for a wide range of high-quality coffees from various parts of the world. It was discovered that there are certain shortcomings in existing online coffee shops, including a lack of personalization, insufficient product information and less intuitive user interfaces.

PERSONA

Nina Jankovic

  • Gender: Female
  • Age: 28
  • Education: Manager
  • Location: Istanbul

“Discovering new and unique coffee beans is like embarking on a flavor adventure for me”

Goals: Nina is passionate about discovering unique and high-quality coffee beans from various regions worldwide. She values convenience and efficiency in her online shopping experience, as her busy schedule leaves little time for in-person shopping. She desire comprehensive product information to make informed purchasing decisions and enhance her coffee brewing experience.

Pain points: Nina finds it frustrating when online coffee shops offer a limited selection of coffee beans, making it challenging to find new and exciting varieties. She often feel overwhelmed by the lack of detailed info about the coffee beans. Nina has encountered difficulties navigating through cluttered and confusing user interfaces on some online platforms.

IMPACT

Imagine, this website is like a kind of magical portal that connects coffee lovers with the finest flavors from around the world. That means it won’t just satisfy customers, but it will give them a real adventurous experience with every sip. And when users experience this premium selection, along with personalized recommendations and easy shopping, you know they will keep coming back. This website will be the epicenter of coffee culture in Istanbul and perhaps even beyond.

WHAT I LEARNED

I realized how important it is to focus on creating a positive user experience on my website. This includes intuitive navigation, clear information, and aesthetically appealing design. I learned how important it is to conduct user research to better understand their needs, preferences, and challenges they face. This allowed me to tailor the content and functionalities of the site to better meet their demands. I realized how useful it is to create user personas to have a clearer understanding of the target audience and their needs. This allowed me to create content and functionalities on the site that are relevant and attractive to my users.
