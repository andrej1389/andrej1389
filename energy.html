OVERVIEW

Energy Fill is a concept app created during my study at Google course. Energy Fill is a comprehensive fitness app that helps you stay active and healthy. With its wide range of features, it allows you to track your steps, running and much more. Whether you are beginner or an experienced athlete, Energy Fill is your ideal partner in achieving your fitness goals.

MY ROLE

In this project, i served as a sole designer, encompassing all phases of the process - from user research to final user experience and interface design. My role was comprehensive, covering research, conceptualization, prototype design and implementation, allowing me to shape every aspect of the project with care and dedication.

PROBLEM STATEMENT

Users are facing complexity and lack of motivation when using fitness apps. Lack of intuitiveness, insufficient personalization and incentives can hinder user engagement and maintaining regular exercise habits. User interaction that simple, intuitive and motivating. Tailored to individual needs and preferences is needed to ensure a positive user experience and long term engagement.

GOAL

Our goal is to provide users with a simple, intuitive and motivating experience through the Energy Fill app. We aim to enable users easily track their activities, set and achieve their fitness goals. Through our app, we want users to feel supported and inspired to maintain healthy lifestyle habits in the long term.

USER RESEARCH

Assumptions after research: Majority of users are assumed want to increase their physical activity to improve their health and well-being. Users prefer apps that are easy to understand and simple to use, without unnecessary complications or complex features. Users prefer non-standard colors that attract more attention. Users want personalized features that suit their individual needs and goals, rather than universal solutions. Users want accurate data about their activities to track their progress and make informed decisions about their fitness program.

IMPACT

I believe that this app, ‘Energy Fill’, will have a significant impact on people looking to improve their health and fitness. Through its simple user experience and motivating features such as step tracking, running and personalized goals, your app will inspire users to become more active and lead healthier lives. Providing accurate activity tracking and supporting users on their journey to achieving their fitness goals will positively influence their physical and mental well-being. Additionally, the use of unconventional colors and intuitive design will enhance the app appeal, attracting a larger user base and encouraging them to stay engaged in the long run.

WHAT I LEARNED

Understanding users needs, preferences and pain points is crucial for the success of an application. Through user research, you can better understand how your application can meet their needs and provide them with value. Users prefer apps that are easy to use and enable them to quickly easily achieve their goals. Focusing on intuitive design and functionality can improve user engagement and satisfaction with their experience. Users want to feel like the application is tailored to them and their needs. Providing personalized features, such as goals and recommendations, can enhance the user experience and encourage long-term engagement. Understanding how your application can positively impact users, such as improving health and well-being, is crucial for identifying the value you provide and communicating with potential users.
