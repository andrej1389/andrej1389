OVERVIEW

KBB is a concept website created during my study at Google course. Kickboxing club KBB is a website intended for members, enthusiasts and prospective students of the kickboxing club.

MY ROLE

In this project, i served as a sole designer, encompassing all phases of the process - from user research to final user experience and interface design. My role was comprehensive, covering research, conceptualization, prototype design and implementation, allowing me to shape every aspect of the project with care and dedication.

PROBLEM STATEMENT

“Kickboxing Club Belgrade” is facing a lack of online presence and an effective means of communication with potential members and existing membership. The current website does not provide sufficient information about the club, its trainers, training fees, training schedule and benefits.

GOAL

Enhance the online presence and efficient communication of Kickboxing Club Belgrade with potential members and existing membership through an improved website that will provide comprehensive information about the club, including details about trainers, training schedules, prices and membership benefits. Additionally, redesign the entire website.

USER RESEARCH

Assumptions before research: the majority of users visit the site to find information about classes and schedules ; users want ti see detailed profiles of trainers and their qualifications ; the majority of users access the site via mobile devices.

Assumptions after research: it was discovered that users also seek information about membership prices and any promotions ; the research showed that users have more trust in trainers with photos or video material ; the majority of users access the site through the desktop version for a full experience and easier navigation.

IMPACT

When i visit your kickboxing site, the first thing that impresses me is its dynamic and modern aesthetic. As soon as i land on the homepage, im immediately drawn to the image and videos of energetic workouts and motivational messages. The navigation is intuitive, allowing me to easily find information about class schedules, membership prices and details about trainers. I particularly like the section with detailed profiles of the trainers, where i can learn more about their experience and qualifications. Overall, your site leaves a positive impression and immediately inspires me to sign up for training session. I think you have successfully captured spirit of your kickboxing community and provided users with everything they need to start their journey towards better health and confidence.

WHAT I LEARNED

I realized how important it is to focus on creating a positive user experience on my website. This includes intuitive navigation, clear information, and aesthetically appealing design. I learned how important it is to conduct user research to better understand their needs, preferences, and challenges they face. This allowed me to tailor the content and functionalities of the site to better meet their demands. I realized how useful it is to create user personas to have a clearer understanding of the target audience and their needs. This allowed me to create content and functionalities on the site that are relevant and attractive to my users.
